Kidambi Srikanth stunned in opening round of US Open badminton

Kidambi Srikanth clinched a historic silver medal in the World Badminton Championships
after suffering a straight-game defeat to Loh Kean Yew of Singapore in the summit clash
to notch up the best ever performance by an Indian in the men's singles in Huelva, Spain on Sunday.

Sport Climbing World Cup Innsbruck 2025

Innsbruck has developed into a real hotspot for climbing enthusiasts and attracts athletes and spectators alike.
That is why the Climbing World Cup will return to Innsbruck-Tirol in 2025.
From 23 to 29 June 2025, for the fifth consecutive year, a Paraclimbing,
Boulder and Lead World Cup are going to take place at Kletterzentrum Innsbruck and showcase the fascinating diversity of sport climbing.
Experience firsthand how the world's best climbers push their limits and master spectacular routes.
More than 500 athletes are expected to compete for the prestigious podium places and showcase their mastery of the sport.
The unique atmosphere at the Kletterzentrum Innsbruck and the breathtaking mountain backdrop make the World Cup an unforgettable experience.
